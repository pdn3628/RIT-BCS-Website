About Us

Bangladesh is a small developing country and as a result is unknown culturally in the global arena. There are many students at RIT who are interested in experiencing a diverse set of cultures from around the globe. As a result, our mission will be to share the culture of Bangladesh and to foster intercultural exchange, fraternity and harmony with other cultural groups at RIT.

Bangladeshi Cultural Society will provide a common platform for the Bangladeshi community in the greater Rochester area while at the same time introducing the RIT community to the history, language, art, music and traditions that has been and will be a part of Bangladesh for a long time.

RIT BCS will host events such as International Mother Language day, Bengali New Year, Victory day and other such events to present the Bangladeshi culture to the RIT campus and also plan on working with other cultural clubs for cultural events.
